Recent Project: MASA Flight Computer V1

As the Avionics Hardware Lead for MASA, I was on the team that designed and built the first version of our Flight Computer that will be used on Limestone. MASA's next rocket. This board is the hub of our distriuted network of DAQ and control boards that are spread around the rocket, and uses a variety of new technologies for MASA. Using an STM32H7 microcontroller, we have circuitry for Ethernet, LoRa WAN Radio, GNSS, as well as a variety of sensors and valve outputs.
