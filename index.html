Recent Project: MASA Thermocouple Board

I recently joined the MASA (Michigan Aeronautical Science Association), as a part of the Avionics subteam. The first project I was assigned was to design a board to read & amplify thermocouples and send the data to the data collection computer. I designed the board in Altium, with a ton of help from Evan Eidt, and finished construction in November 2023. There have been a few issues, mainly as a result of my lack of knowledge and experience, but it seems to be providing some value, and was a great learning experience.
